# 🍱 TiffinWala – Smart Subscription-Based Meal Management Platform

## 🚀 Tagline
A smart, subscription-driven meal management system designed to optimize food consumption, reduce wastage, and empower vendors with real-time insights.

---

## ❗ Problem Statement

Traditional mess and tiffin systems suffer from major inefficiencies:

- ❌ **Vague Cancellation Process**  
  Users inform vendors manually or too late, leading to food wastage.

- ❌ **Static Subscription Models**  
  Fixed meal plans without flexibility (no pause, no tracking, no control).

- ❌ **Lack of Real-Time Data**  
  Vendors prepare food based on assumptions rather than actual demand.

- ❌ **No Transparency or Tracking**  
  No clear record of meals consumed, skipped, or remaining.

- ❌ **Poor User Experience**  
  No centralized system for managing subscriptions, menus, or payments.

---

## 💡 Solution / Core Pillars

TiffinWala transforms traditional systems into a **smart, automated, and data-driven platform**:

### 🔐 Role-Based Access System
- Admin, Vendor, and User roles
- Secure authentication and access control

### 📦 Dynamic Subscription Engine
- Flexible meal plans (meals + validity + type)
- Track:
  - Meals consumed
  - Meals skipped
  - Meals remaining
- Auto-expiry and pause subscription feature

### ⏱️ Smart Cancellation System
- Configurable cutoff time per vendor
- Auto-lock cancellations after deadline
- Real-time update of meal counts

### 📊 Vendor Intelligence Dashboard
- Meals to prepare (after cancellations)
- Active users and revenue insights
- Demand trends and peak days

### 🍽️ Menu Management System
- Daily menu (breakfast/lunch/dinner)
- Weekly preview for users
- Menu-based meal skipping

### 🔔 Notification System
- Meal reminders
- Cancellation alerts
- Menu updates
- Subscription expiry notifications

### 💳 Payment & Billing System
- Subscription purchase tracking
- Payment status (Paid/Pending)
- Basic invoice management

### ⭐ Ratings & Feedback
- Users can rate vendors
- Feedback for service improvement
- Vendor rating visibility

### 🧾 Daily Meal Ledger
- Tracks:
  - Opted meals
  - Cancelled meals
  - Final meals served
- Enables analytics and transparency

### 📴 Pause Subscription Feature
- Users can pause plans for specific duration
- Automatic extension of validity

### 📍 Location-Based Vendor Discovery
- Find vendors nearby
- Filter by price, rating, and meal type
